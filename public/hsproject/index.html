What is this website?

This website was created to showcase my learning throughout my English 4 semester. After starting with a very weak understanding of web design, I have taught myself how to code in many of the different languages required, I have learned about all the different types of website layouts, and I have begun stepping into the more complex Javascript and PHP languages. I plan to include research regarding web design careers in the near future. Web design is a passion of mine and many, many hours have been put into my various websites. I hope you enjoy it!
